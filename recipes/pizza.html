Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Red Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Red Pepper Flakes optional

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven and cast iron pan at 425°F
  2. Roll out dough in a circle onto a lightly floured surface
  3. Spread a think layer of sauce onto the dough
  4. Sprinkle the Mozzarella on top of the sauce
  5. Liberally add the pepperoni all over the pizza
  6. Place the pizza in the greased cast iron pan
  7. Bake until desired crispiness is acheived
