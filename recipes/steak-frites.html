Ingredients

  • Hangar Steak
  • Thin Cut Fries

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oil to 300℉
  2. Sear steak until dark golden brown
  3. Finish Steak in oven until medium rare
  4. Gently deep fry the fries
  5. Rest steak for 5 mins
  6. Slice Steak and plate with large heap of fries
